About

I'm an independent engineer and researcher in AI safety and large language model (LLM) interpretability. My recent work introduced and open-sourced a cross‑lingual sparse autoencoder architecture that improves feature quality and reduces language-specific biases, advancing transparency and fairness. I'm especially interested in evaluating and deploying new risk-reducing LLM interpretability methods, and in bridging technical alignment research with governance and deployment decisions.

Lagrange Interpolation

The internet's first interactive visualization of the Lagrange interpolation polynomial based on user-defined points on a 2D graph. It dynamically recalculates the interpolating polynomial in real-time as points are added, moved, or removed.
